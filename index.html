Welcome

I am completing my Ph.D. at the Katholieke Universiteit Leuven with an expected degree date of May 2019. My research is dedicated to the development of tomographic image/data processing algorithms. Specific topics that I have investigated are: novel beam hardening correction algorithms for cone-beam CT, a novel x-ray spectral estimation scheme that does not require scattering reduction, and imaging performance evaluation and quantitative validation.

My passion is to advance tomographic image processing by combining machine learning techniques and the mathematical modeling of imaging physics.
