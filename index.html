Welcome

Wenchao Cao is completing her Ph.D. at the Katholieke Universiteit Leuven with an expected degree date of May 2019. Her research is dedicated to the development of tomographic image/data processing algorithms. Specific topics investigated by Wenchao have included novel beam hardening correction algorithms for CBCT, a novel X-ray spectral estimation scheme that does not require scattering reduction, and imaging performance evaluation and quantitative validation.

Her passion is to advance tomographic image processing by combining machine learning techniques and the mathematical modeling of imaging physics.
